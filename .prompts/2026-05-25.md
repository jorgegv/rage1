## Task 2: Evaluate 1-bit tile storage

- We have previously reached the conclusion that graphics assets in CPC mode 1 would occupy about 2x the size of ZX versions, due to 2 bits per pixel
- Evaluate if it would be interesting to have a special CPC graphics mode in which the Tiles can be stored as 8x8 1-bpp tiles (i.e. same UDGs as ZX version) and converted on the fly when outputting to screen, to 2bpp with a custom tile-print routine. Since the tiles are mostly static and not frequently updated (normally on enter/exit screen), the performance hit may be worth it.
- We would do this for tiles only. Sprites would use the needed 2bpp storage for efficiency and speed, and also there are less assets
- What's your take on this?
